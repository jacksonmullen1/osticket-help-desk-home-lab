# Printer Troubleshooting

## Purpose

This article outlines the standard troubleshooting process for printers that stop printing or have stalled print jobs.

---

## Symptoms

Users may report:

* Printer not printing
* Jobs stuck in the print queue
* Printer appears offline
* Print jobs disappear without printing

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verify printer power and network connectivity.
2. Confirm the printer is online.
3. Open the Windows print queue.
4. Clear any stalled print jobs.
5. Restart the Print Spooler service.
6. Print a Windows test page.
7. Confirm successful printing from the user's application.

---

## Resolution

The stalled print queue was cleared, the Print Spooler service restarted, and successful printing was verified.

---

## Notes

If the issue persists after restarting the Print Spooler, verify printer drivers and network connectivity before escalating.
